Address Book

The address book allows you to store contact details of users who may be interested in the calculations. To open the Address Book, simply click on the email menu (ALT+m) and then click Address Book (ALT+a).

Adding a Contact

A contact can be created by clicking on the New button in the side menu. A dialog will then appear asking for the contact's details - fill this in, click "ok" and you're done.

Editing a Contact

If you realise you have made a mistake, or a Contact changes their email address, the Contact can be edited by selecting the user in the table. Next click the edit button. The same dialog as before will appear, just make the alterations, and you're done.

Removing a Contact

To delete a contact, simply select them in the table, and press the delete button in the side menu.
