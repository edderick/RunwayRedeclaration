Calculations View

The calculations view gives a textual representation of the calculations. It offers a breakdown of the calculations so that they can be checked by hand by a qualified operator. To show the calculations view, click on the View menu (ALT+v) and then choose a panel to display it in (ALT-t for Top Panel, Alt-b for the Bottom Panel). Finally select Calculations.

If you wish to fill the entire screen with the calculations, select the other panel and set it to display none.

Note: There must be an obstacle selected for meaningful calculations to be shown
