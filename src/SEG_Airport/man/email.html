Sending Email

To send an email, the desired recipients must first be added to the Address Book. (See the Address Book Manual)

Once the contacts have been added to the Address Book, click on the email menu (ALT+m), and select Send Email (ALT+s). A dilaog will appear containing a list of Contatcs; Select the contacts to send the email to by checking the checkbox next to their name.

Finally click the send link to send an email to the selected results

Note: The email sent will be the same as the calculations View
